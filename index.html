A few words about this website

This webpage illustrates my path to becoming a qualified data scientists. I started out with minimal experience in coding and zero background in data science. It is my wish that this page can be the testimony of preserverance and help others who wish to enter the wonderful world of modern data analytics.

In these pages, you can find the data projects, educational resources, and some personal insights. These resources have helped my greatly, and I wish they would be equally helpful to you as well.
